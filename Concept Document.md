# Style
At all times when making anything audiovisual, consider that the game should be grainy and disturbing. Moving actors will be drawn in 2D, a la 'Don't Starve', but the sprites should be drawn in a 3D world (think Pok√©mon B&W)

Drawing further from Don't Starve, and also Fran Bow as artistic inspirations would be effective, as these styles are really good at portraying eldritch horror, and are better suited for Junji Ito's work.
